Our Story

Founded in 2019, Sumato Consulting emerged from a simple yet ambitious vision: to help businesses leverage technology as a competitive advantage rather than just a necessary cost. Our founder saw that many organizations were struggling to keep pace with rapid technological changes and wanted to bridge the gap between business objectives and technical implementations.

The name "Sumato" draws inspiration from the concept of "smart" implementation of technology that truly serves business needs. We've grown into a comprehensive IT solutions/services provider serving clients across various industries throughout Canada and beyond.

Throughout our journey, we've maintained our core commitment to understanding each client's unique challenges and delivering tailored solutions that drive real business value. Today, Sumato Consulting stands as a trusted partner for organizations seeking to transform their operations through innovative technology solutions.
