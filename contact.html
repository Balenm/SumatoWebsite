Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

How do I get started with Sumato Consulting's services?

Getting started is easy! Simply fill out the contact form on this page or call us directly. One of our consultants will reach out to schedule an initial consultation to understand your needs and discuss how we can help. This initial consultation is free and comes with no obligation.

Do you offer services for small businesses or only enterprise clients?

We work with organizations of all sizes, from small businesses to large enterprises. Our solutions are scalable and can be tailored to fit your specific needs and budget. We believe that businesses of any size can benefit from the right technology solutions.

How long does a typical IT project with Sumato take to complete?

Project timelines vary depending on scope, complexity, and specific requirements. Small projects might take a few weeks, while larger digital transformation initiatives could span several months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline estimate for your specific project. We pride ourselves on delivering on time and within budget.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance options after project completion. Our team can provide training, documentation, regular check-ins, and technical support to ensure your new systems continue to run smoothly and effectively. We can tailor a support package that meets your specific needs and budget.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have experience across numerous industries, including healthcare, finance, manufacturing, retail, education, and non-profit. Our team brings a wealth of cross-industry knowledge, allowing us to implement best practices and innovative solutions regardless of your sector. Each industry has unique challenges, and we've developed specialized expertise to address those specific needs.
